1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow user recognition and improve the functionality and analytics of the site.

2. WHAT COOKIES DO WE USE?

2.1. Necessary cookies

They are mandatory for the proper functioning of the site and do not require consent.

2.2. Analytical cookies

We use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Microsoft Clarity

These tools collect information such as:

  • pages visited
  • time spent on the site
  • interactions (clicks, scrolling, etc.)

The data is anonymized and used to improve the site.

2.3. Marketing cookies

We use:

  • Google Ads
  • Facebook Pixel
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag

They are used for:

  • displaying relevant ads
  • remarketing
  • measuring campaign effectiveness

3. LEGAL BASIS

The use of cookies (except for necessary ones) is carried out only after explicit consent from the User according to:

  • Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)
  • Electronic Communications Act

4. CONSENT MANAGEMENT

Upon first visiting the site, the User sees a cookie banner that allows:

  • accept all cookies
  • refusal of all (except mandatory)
  • selection of specific categories
  • The user can change their consent at any time.

The user can change their consent at any time.

5. SHELF LIFE

Cookies are stored for different periods depending on their type:

Cookies may be set by external providers (e.g. Google, Meta, LinkedIn, Microsoft). These providers may process data in accordance with their privacy policies.

7. HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

You can:
