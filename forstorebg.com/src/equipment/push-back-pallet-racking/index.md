When loading, each new pallet pushes the previous one back, and when unloading, the pallets automatically move forward by gravity.<p>The system operates on the LIFO (Last In, First Out) principle, with loading and picking of goods taking place from the same work lane. This eliminates the need for forklifts to enter the racks and significantly speeds up operations.<p>Push-back systems allow multiple pallets to be stored in a single row (typically 2 to 6 deep), with each row containing a different item. This makes them an excellent solution for warehouses with limited space and medium turnover.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Push-Back Pallet Racking" title="Push-Back Pallet Racking" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/assets/images/equipment/push-back-pallet-racking/push-back-pallet-racking1.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>

This allows better utilization of both the area and the height of the warehouse.<p>Working with them is extremely fast and efficient, as all operations are performed from one side. The lack of forklift entry into the structure reduces maneuvering time and increases safety during operation.<p>The system combines high storage density with good selectivity – each line is independent and can contain a different product without disturbing the arrangement of the others.<p>Push-back racks require minimal maintenance and are suitable for operation in a variety of conditions, including refrigerated and freezer warehouses, thanks to their sturdy construction and reliable mechanisms.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Push-Back Pallet Racking" title="Push-Back Pallet Racking" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/assets/images/equipment/push-back-pallet-racking/push-back-pallet-racking2.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>
