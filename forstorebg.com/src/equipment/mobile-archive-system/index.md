<div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container">

</div>
</div>
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            The mobile archive system is a modern solution for document storage,
            which allows for significant optimization of the available space. It
            consists of mobile racks that move on rails, thus eliminating the
            need for multiple fixed corridors. This makes it extremely suitable
            for rooms with limited space.
          </p>
<p>
            The main advantage of this type of system is the ability to increase
            storage capacity without the need to expand the room. The compact
            arrangement of the racks makes use of almost all of the available
            space, which is especially important for archives with a large
            volume of documentation.
          </p>
<p>
            Mobile archiving systems are widely used in various sectors,
            including administration, finance, healthcare, and cultural
            institutions. They provide not only efficiency, but also good
            organization and protection of stored information.
          </p>
</div>

<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            Mobile archive systems offer extremely efficient use of space, with
            the ability to increase storage capacity by up to twice that of
            standard solutions, making them ideal for organizations that deal
            with large volumes of documentation and have limited space.
          </p>
<p>
            Another important aspect is the convenience of work. The system
            allows quick and easy access to all archives, without having to go
            through multiple corridors. The movement of the racks is designed to
            be smooth and safe, which reduces the effort of the staff.
          </p>
<p>
            In addition, mobile systems offer a high level of security. They can
            be equipped with locking mechanisms and access control systems,
            ensuring the protection of important and sensitive information.
          </p>

<section class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection" id="technology">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle">
<div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container">
<span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Types of systems</span>
</div>
</div>
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            Mobile archive systems are available in various versions to meet the
            specific needs of users. The most common are manual systems, which
            are operated by a mechanical crank and are suitable for smaller
            spaces.
          </p>
<p>
            These systems are economical and reliable, providing basic
            functionality without the need for electrical power. They are the
            preferred choice for smaller archives or rooms with moderate
            traffic.
          </p>
<p>
            On the other hand, electrical systems offer a higher level of
            comfort and automation. They are controlled by buttons or
            intelligent panels and are suitable for large archive bases where
            quick and easy access to a large volume of information is required.
          </p>
</div>

<section class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection Services-module__3bADUG__reverse" id="comparison">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle">
<div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container">
<span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Comparison with static racks</span>
</div>
</div>
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            Static shelving is a traditional storage solution where each row is
            separated by a permanent aisle. This results in a significant loss
            of available space, especially in larger archives.
          </p>
<p>
            Mobile archive systems eliminate this disadvantage by using only one
            working aisle. This allows for a significant increase in storage
            capacity without the need for additional space.
          </p>
<p>
            Although the initial investment for mobile systems is higher, they
            offer better efficiency, better organization, and long-term savings,
            making them the better solution in most cases.
          </p>
</div>
