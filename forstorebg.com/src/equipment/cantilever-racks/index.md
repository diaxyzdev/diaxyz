<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            Cantilever racks are an effective solution for storing and handling
            long and bulky loads, such as metal profiles, plastic pipes, timber
            and other products with non-standard lengths. Thanks to their open
            design, they provide direct access to every position, which
            facilitates both manual handling of lighter loads and work with
            forklifts and other lifting equipment for heavier loads.
          </p>
<p>
            The system consists of stable bases, vertical columns and cantilever
            arms on which the materials are placed. This structure is extremely
            strong, durable and flexible, which allows easy adaptation to
            different types of warehouses and larger products. The cantilever
            racks can be configured according to specific load capacity and size
            requirements, and there is also the option of a mobile system that
            optimizes the use of warehouse space.
          </p>

            service it.

            The system is built from modular elements, which allows for quick

            safety and the ability to be used in both indoor and outdoor
            warehouse areas.

            Cantilever racks are widely used in warehouses and production
            facilities where long, bulky or difficult-to-palletize loads are
            stored. They are particularly suitable for storing pipes, metal and
            aluminum profiles, beams, timber, boards and other long products.
            Thanks to its design, the system allows for efficient arrangement of
            products with specific dimensions that cannot be easily stored on
            standard pallets.
          </p>
<p>
            This type of racking is often used in industrial warehouses,
            logistics centers, manufacturing plants and building materials
            stores. They provide convenient and safe storage of goods of
            different weights and sizes, allowing quick access to each product
            and better organization of the warehouse space.
          </p>
