Instead of building a separate structure of columns and beams, on which to subsequently install racks, here the racks themselves take all the loads - both from the stored goods and from external factors such as wind, snow and seismic effects. This makes them one of the most effective solutions in modern logistics.</p><p>This type of warehouse is distinguished by the ability to reach significant heights, often over 30-40 meters, which allows optimal use of the available area. The absence of internal columns enables maximum utilization of space and creates conditions for high storage density. This is especially important for companies with limited grounds but high capacity requirements.</p><p>Self-supporting warehouses are often combined with automated goods management systems, making them an ideal solution for modern logistics centers. Since a separate supporting structure is not required, less material is used and construction time is reduced. This results in a faster return on investment and a lower overall project budget.</p><p>Another significant advantage is the maximum use of space. Thanks to the high construction and the absence of internal partitions, a much larger capacity is achieved compared to standard warehouses. This allows storage of large quantities of goods in a relatively small area, which is critical for industries with high turnover.</p><p>Self-supporting warehouses are also extremely suitable for automation. They can be easily integrated with warehouse cranes, shuttle systems and transport lines. These include warehouse cranes and fully automated pallet storage and retrieval processes. This solution is suitable for large logistics centers where high speed and precision are required.</p><p>There are also specialized self-supporting warehouses for refrigerated and freezer conditions. In traditional buildings, a supporting structure is built first, then the racks are installed. In self-supporting warehouses, these two functions are combined, resulting in a more efficient use of resources.</p><p>In addition, self-supporting warehouses allow for significantly greater height and capacity. This makes them more suitable for companies that work with large volumes of goods and need a compact but highly efficient solution.</p><p>In the long term, these types of warehouses offer a better return on investment, thanks to lower operating costs, automation capabilities and higher efficiency of warehouse operations.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper" style="transform:scale(0.95)"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Самоносещи складове" title="Самоносещи складове" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" style="color:transparent" src="/images/oborudvane/samonoseshti-skladove/samonoseshti-skladove4.webp"></div></div></div></div></section><section id="applications" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection "><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle" style="opacity:0;transform:translateY(60px)"><div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container"><span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Areas of application</span></div></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid" style="opacity:0;transform:translateY(60px)"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock"><p>A self-supporting warehouse is an excellent choice when available space is limited but maximum storage capacity is needed. The height development of the warehouse allows to compensate for the lack of horizontal space and to achieve high efficiency.</p><p>This solution is particularly suitable for companies with intensive logistics processes and a large turnover of goods. The ability to integrate with automated systems allows for significant acceleration of operations and reduction of labor costs.</p><p>Self-supporting warehouses are also preferred in the construction of modern industrial and distribution centers, where a combination of high efficiency, quick construction and long-term sustainability is sought.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper" style="transform:scale(0.95)"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Самоносещи складове" title="Самоносещи складове" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" style="color:transparent" src="/images/oborudvane/samonoseshti-skladove/samonoseshti-skladove5.webp"></div></div></div></div></section><section id="components" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection Services-module__3bADUG__reverse"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle" style="opacity:0;transform:translateY(60px)"><div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container"><span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Main components of the system</span></div></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid" style="opacity:0;transform:translateY(60px)"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock"><p>The main element of the self-supporting warehouse is the rack structure, which performs a double function - it stores the goods and at the same time carries the entire building. High-strength steels and precise engineering calculation are used.</p><p>Roof and facade panels are added to the structure, which are mounted directly on the racks.
