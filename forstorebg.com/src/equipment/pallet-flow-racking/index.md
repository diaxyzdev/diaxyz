They are loaded on one side of the rack and moved by gravity to the opposite side, where they are shipped.<p>The system works on the FIFO (First In, First Out) principle, which ensures that the first pallet stored is the first retrieved. This makes it particularly suitable for goods with a short shelf life or high turnover.<p>Thanks to the lack of internal aisles and the possibility of deep storage, this system significantly increases warehouse capacity and optimizes work processes.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Gravity pallet racks" title="Gravity pallet racks" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/images/oborudvane/pallet-flow-racking/pallet-flow-racking1.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>



<section id="advantages" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection Services-module__3bADUG__reverse">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle"><div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container"><span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Key benefits</span></div></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock"><p>Pallet Flow systems provide extremely efficient use of warehouse space, as they allow for the storage of a large number of pallets in depth without the need for multiple aisles. This results in a significant increase in capacity compared to standard racks.<p>Organizing goods using the FIFO principle improves inventory control and minimizes the risk of stockouts or expirations. This is key for industries such as food and pharmaceuticals.<p>The workflow is faster and more efficient as loading and unloading are done from different sides. This eliminates cross-flows and reduces forklift maneuvering time.<p>An additional advantage is the automatic movement of the pallets - when the first one is removed, the others move forward on their own, which speeds up the processing of goods and reduces the need for additional operations.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Gravity pallet racks" title="Gravity pallet racks" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/images/oborudvane/pallet-flow-racking/pallet-flow-racking2.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>
