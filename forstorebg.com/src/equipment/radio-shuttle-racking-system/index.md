    <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
      <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
        <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
          <p>
            The radio shuttle system is extremely suitable for warehouses with
            large volumes and high intensity of cargo turnover.
          </p>
          <p>
            It is widely used in logistics centers, manufacturing plants and
            distribution warehouses where large quantities of pallets of the
            same item are stored.
          </p>
          <p>
            The system is particularly effective in FMCG, buffer zones for
            shipping, food industry and cold storage, production and
            intermediate storage.
          </p>
          <p>
            Thanks to its high density, it is an excellent solution for limited
            storage space where maximum volume utilization is critical.
          </p>
          <p>
            The process begins with the pallet being fed to the input area by
            conveyor or forklift. The automated system or operator then
            positions the shuttle in the appropriate channel.
          </p>
          <p>
            The shuttle picks up the pallet, lifts it slightly and transports it
            to the deepest free position in the channel, where it leaves it on
            the rails. When removing, the process is carried out in reverse
            order.
          </p>
          <p>
            In fully automated systems, the movement of the shuttle is
            coordinated by software (WMS/WCS), which manages all warehouse
            operations and ensures optimal flow of goods.
          </p>
