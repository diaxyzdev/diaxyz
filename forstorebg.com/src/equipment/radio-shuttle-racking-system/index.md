It takes over the tasks of inserting and removing pallets, eliminating the need for forklifts to enter the racks.<p>The system combines high storage density with process automation, leading to a significant increase in productivity and optimal use of available space.<p>The radio shuttle can operate both in semi-automatic mode (with forklifts) and in fully automated systems with transfer carts or stacker cranes, making it an extremely flexible solution for various warehouse needs.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Radio shuttle racking system" title="Radio shuttle racking system" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/images/oborudvane/radio-shuttle-racking-system/radio-shuttle-racking-system1.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>

They allow for much greater storage capacity thanks to deep storage channels and reduced aisles.<p>In addition, the system increases the speed of pallet processing, as the shuttle moves quickly inside the channels and reduces the time for loading and unloading operations.<p>Another key plus is safety – since forklifts do not enter the racks, the risk of accidents and damage is significantly lower.<p>The radio shuttle system also allows for continuous operation with high productivity, reduction of human errors, operation in various temperature conditions, including cold storage, management and control through a WMS system.<p><p></p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Radio shuttle racking system" title="Radio shuttle racking system" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/images/oborudvane/radio-shuttle-racking-system/radio-shuttle-racking-system2.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>



<section id="applications" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceSection">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__contentTitle"><div class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__container"><span class="SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__subtitle SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__big SectionTitle-module__Svbyca__orange">Areas of application</span></div></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock"><p>The radio shuttle system is extremely suitable for warehouses with large volumes and high intensity of cargo turnover.<p>It is widely used in logistics centers, manufacturing plants and distribution warehouses where large quantities of pallets of the same item are stored.<p>The system is particularly effective in FMCG, buffer zones for shipping, food industry and cold storage, production and intermediate storage.<p>Thanks to its high density, it is an excellent solution for limited storage space where maximum volume utilization is critical.<p>The process begins with the pallet being fed to the input area by conveyor or forklift. The automated system or operator then positions the shuttle in the appropriate channel.<p>The shuttle picks up the pallet, lifts it slightly and transports it to the deepest free position in the channel, where it leaves it on the rails. When removing, the process is carried out in reverse order.<p>In fully automated systems, the movement of the shuttle is coordinated by software (WMS/WCS), which manages all warehouse operations and ensures optimal flow of goods.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Radio shuttle racking system" title="Radio shuttle racking system" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/images/oborudvane/radio-shuttle-racking-system/radio-shuttle-racking-system3.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>
