It uses inclined roller conveyors on which cartons move automatically from the loading area to the picking area under the influence of gravity.<p>Goods are loaded from the back of the rack and operators retrieve them from the front, and as one carton is removed, the next one automatically takes its place. This allows for a continuous flow of materials and minimizes order processing time.<p>The system works on the FIFO (First In, First Out) principle, which ensures proper product rotation and is particularly suitable for goods with an expiration date or high turnover.</p></div><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imageWrapper"><div class="Services-module__3bADUG__imagesContainer"><img alt="Gravity system for cartons and boxes" title="Gravity system for cartons and boxes" loading="lazy" width="800" height="600" decoding="async" data-nimg="1" class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceImage" src="/assets/images/equipment/gravity-system-for-cartons-and-boxes/gravity-system-for-cartons-and-boxes1.webp" style="color:transparent"></div></div></div></div></section>
