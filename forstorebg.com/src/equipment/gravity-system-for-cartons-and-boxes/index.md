<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            The Carton Flow Rack is a dynamic warehouse system designed for the
            storage and picking of small to medium-sized loads. It uses inclined
            roller conveyors on which cartons move automatically from the
            loading area to the picking area under the influence of gravity.
          </p>
<p>
            Goods are loaded from the back of the rack and operators retrieve
            them from the front, and as one carton is removed, the next one
            automatically takes its place. This allows for a continuous flow of
            materials and minimizes order processing time.
          </p>
<p>
            The system works on the FIFO (First In, First Out) principle, which
            ensures proper product rotation and is particularly suitable for
            goods with an expiration date or high turnover.
          </p>
</div>

<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
<div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
<p>
            Gravity box systems significantly increase the efficiency of
            warehouse operations. Thanks to the automatic movement of goods,
            operators do not have to make unnecessary movements, which leads to
            faster order processing and lower labor costs.
          </p>
<p>
            In addition, the system allows for much better use of warehouse
            space through high-density storage. Compared to standard racks, a
            significant increase in capacity and better organization of SKU
            items can be achieved.
          </p>
<p>
            Another key plus is the separation of loading and picking areas,
            which eliminates congestion and allows multiple operators to work
            simultaneously without conflict.
          </p>
<p>
            The system also provides excellent visibility and control over
            inventory, which facilitates warehouse management and reduces the
            risk of errors.
          </p>

            items.

            goods is critically important.

            pallet racks, pick-to-light systems or conveyors, making it
            extremely flexible when building modern logistics centers.
