      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__container">
      <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__serviceGrid">
        <div class="Services-module__3bADUG__textBlock">
          <p>
            When choosing a storage system, one of the main differences is
            between traditional static shelving and modern mobile solutions.
            Although at first glance they perform the same function, their
            efficiency and space utilization differ significantly.
          </p>
          <p>
            In static warehouses, each row of racks is separated by fixed access
            corridors. This means that a large part of the area remains unused,
            as it is intended solely for the passage of equipment and operators.
            As a result, the actual capacity of the warehouse is limited.
          </p>
          <p>
            On the other hand, in mobile rack warehouses, only one working aisle
            is used, which opens up where access is needed. All other rows are
            compactly packed together, allowing for significantly better
            utilization of the available space.
          </p>
          <p>
            The difference is most noticeable in capacity and efficiency. While
            with static systems, increasing volume often requires expanding the
            warehouse, mobile racking allows for almost double the storage in
            the same area.
          </p>
        </div>
