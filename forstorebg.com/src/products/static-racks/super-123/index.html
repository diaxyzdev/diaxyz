System description

The Super 1-2-3 Shelving System is a versatile manual shelving system designed to meet a wide range of warehouse and retail needs. The system is designed to provide quick assembly, high load capacity and maximum configuration flexibility.

The construction is boltless, allowing easy assembly without the use of special tools. The shelves can be moved quickly and easily thanks to the 33 mm perforation pitch, which allows for optimal use of space and adaptation to different sizes of stored products.

All components are made of high-strength structural steel, certified to the EN 10204 standard, and the galvanized coating provides long-lasting corrosion protection and guarantees reliability over many years of operation.

The modular design allows for the construction of rack configurations with different sizes and additional accessories, including corner modules under 90°, which optimize the use of warehouse space.

Key benefits

The Super 1-2-3 shelving system offers a number of practical and design advantages that make it a preferred choice for many warehouse and commercial applications.

One of its main advantages is the quick and easy installation, thanks to the boltless design. This significantly reduces installation time and allows for easy expansion or configuration changes if necessary.

The system features a high load capacity, allowing the storage of both light and medium-weight loads. The use of high-strength steel guarantees stability and reliability even under intensive use.

A great advantage is the flexibility in configuration. The modular structure allows the construction of racks in different sizes and configurations, and the wide range of accessories allows for adaptation to the specific needs of the warehouse or commercial space.

The galvanized coating provides long-lasting corrosion protection, making the system suitable for various working environments and ensuring that its appearance and functionality are maintained over time.

An additional advantage is the modern industrial design, which allows the system to be used not only in warehouses, but also in commercial premises, offices and public spaces.

Areas of application

Thanks to its flexibility and strength, Super 1-2-3 finds application in a wide range of environments and industries.

The system is an excellent solution for manual warehouses where cartons, containers, spare parts or various types of small and medium-sized goods are stored.

It is also suitable for commercial establishments and shops, where in addition to functionality, a good visual appearance of the equipment is required.

It is also often used in offices, archives and document warehouses where an organized and easily accessible storage system is needed.

Thanks to the durable construction and galvanized coating, the system can also be used in production facilities, workshops and industrial warehouses, as well as in domestic spaces such as garages and workshops.
