System description

Conventional Pallet Racking is the best solution for warehouses where it is necessary to store a wide variety of items on pallets.

A wide range of profiles and accessories ensure full adaptation to all loads and specific requirements.

The location and height of the racks are determined by the characteristics of the forklifts used, the pallets that need to be stored, and the dimensions of the room in which they will be built.

Key benefits

Direct access to each pallet – ensures quick and easy operation, with each pallet position accessible directly from the work aisle.

Maximum efficiency of warehouse space – the system adapts to the height and configuration of the warehouse to optimally use the available space.

Flexibility for different loads – suitable for pallets, containers, cartons, barrels and other loads of different sizes and weights.

Modular and expandable design – levels can be adjusted, and the system is easily expanded as storage needs increase.

Ideal for picking operations - allows for quick order picking from both lower levels and higher positions with appropriate equipment.

Areas of application

Warehouses with a wide variety of SKUs – pallet racks are an ideal solution for storing and organizing a wide variety of products, providing easy access to every warehouse position.

Multi-client warehouses (3PL) – the system allows for effective management of goods from different clients and is an excellent solution for logistics centers with high cargo turnover dynamics.

Large and small warehouses – thanks to their modular design, pallet racks can be adapted for both compact warehouses and large logistics centers.

Storage of various types of cargo – in addition to standard pallets, the system can be configured to store containers, cartons, barrels, coils and other cargo.

Warehouse space optimization – solutions such as double-depth racks allow for increased storage capacity while maintaining access to goods.

Order picking – the lower levels of the racks can be configured for quick and convenient manual order picking.
