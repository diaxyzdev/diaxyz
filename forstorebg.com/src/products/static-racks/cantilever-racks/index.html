System description

Cantilever racks are an effective solution for storing and handling long and bulky loads, such as metal profiles, plastic pipes, timber and other products with non-standard lengths. Thanks to their open design, they provide direct access to every position, which facilitates both manual handling of lighter loads and work with forklifts and other lifting equipment for heavier loads.

The system consists of stable bases, vertical columns and cantilever arms on which the materials are placed. This structure is extremely strong, durable and flexible, which allows easy adaptation to different types of warehouses and larger products. The cantilever racks can be configured according to specific load capacity and size requirements, and there is also the option of a mobile system that optimizes the use of warehouse space.

Key benefits

Cantilever racks are highly flexible and can easily adapt to the storage of various long and bulky loads of various sizes and weights. The open structure provides direct access and good visibility to each product, making it easy to quickly find and service it.

The system is built from modular elements, which allows for quick installation and easy expansion if necessary. The cantilever racks can also be configured as a mobile system for better use of warehouse space, and the sturdy construction guarantees stability, safety and the ability to be used in both indoor and outdoor warehouse areas.

Areas of application

Cantilever racks are widely used in warehouses and production facilities where long, bulky or difficult-to-palletize loads are stored. They are particularly suitable for storing pipes, metal and aluminum profiles, beams, timber, boards and other long products. Thanks to its design, the system allows for efficient arrangement of products with specific dimensions that cannot be easily stored on standard pallets.

This type of racking is often used in industrial warehouses, logistics centers, manufacturing plants and building materials stores. They provide convenient and safe storage of goods of different weights and sizes, allowing quick access to each product and better organization of the warehouse space.
